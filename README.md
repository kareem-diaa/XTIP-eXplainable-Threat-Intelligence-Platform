## What is XTIP?

XTIP is a **Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI) and Attack Surface Management (ASM)** platform built to help security teams move from reactive incident response to proactive, intelligence-driven defense.

Most organizations drown in raw threat data they can't act on. XTIP changes that by aggregating, correlating, and **explaining** threat intelligence in context — giving analysts the *why* behind threats, not just the *what*.

> **"Explainable" is the key word.** XTIP doesn't just surface threats — it tells your team what they mean, why they matter, and what to do next.

---

## Core Capabilities

### 🔍 Threat Intelligence Aggregation
Collect and normalize threat data from multiple sources into a single, structured feed — eliminating alert fatigue and tool sprawl.

### 🌐 Attack Surface Management
Continuously map your organization's external-facing assets, identify exposure points, and track changes over time before attackers do.

### 🧠 Explainable Intelligence
Every alert and indicator comes with context: attribution, tactics (MITRE ATT&CK mapping), severity rationale, and recommended actions — not just raw IOCs.

### 📊 Unified SOC Dashboard
A single pane of glass for your security posture: prioritized threats, asset inventory, exposure trends, and actionable remediation guidance.

## The Problem We're Solving

Egypt and the broader MENA region face a growing volume of sophisticated cyber threats — yet most organizations in the region rely on fragmented, international tools that lack local context and integrations.

XTIP is built **from the region, for the region** — with threat intelligence that reflects the actual threat landscape facing Egyptian and MENA enterprises, and a platform architecture designed for local deployment requirements.

---

## Technology Approach

XTIP is built on a foundation of:

- **Structured threat data** following industry standards (STIX/TAXII, MITRE ATT&CK)
- **Automated collection pipelines** from open and commercial intelligence sources
- **Correlation engines** that connect disparate signals into coherent threat pictures
- **API-first architecture** for integration with existing SIEM and SOAR tooling
